About Nazarite Harmony Choir.

~ ~ The Meaning And History Of The Nazarite Harmonies.~ ~

The word Nazarite logicaly according to the dictates that life requires means a Jew bound by a vow to lave the hair uncut,to abstain from wine and soft drinks and to practice extra-ordinary purity of life and devotion.

Spiritually,the word Nazarite was at first derived from Amos chapter 2 verse 11 (AMOS 2 : 11) to mean a chosen person,nation,or group of individuals that will stand faithfully in serving God no matter the situation.whether people worship him or not but they will still stand to represent God through their service to God.

Therefore,Nazarite harmony also as a singing group have accepted to stand firm in the worship of God no matter the situation.
