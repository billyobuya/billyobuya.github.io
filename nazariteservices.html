Services Offered Within Nazarite Harmonies Choir.

Nazarite Harmonies as a choir offers The following Services Within the group. They Include:

~ ~ Song Services. ~ ~

This has always been the main intention of the Group since Being a choir, it has mainly been more concerned with Singing.By the Grace Of God, all The singing sessions have always been a blessing and a success.

All the singing sessions have always been accompanied by Prayers before and also after singing.



~ ~ Bible Study. ~ ~

This has always been also a major Booster towards our spiritual growth not only as a singing group but also as individuals within the group since it has always been a blessing to us through the studies.

It has always involved several Bible verses and spiritual Quotes which May be of great benefit to us not only as a singing group or as individuals but also as a human Being.



~ ~ Encouragements. ~ ~

This has always been done through reading bible verses with regards to several situations which may affect individuals according to the dictates that life requires.

This has always been also a major boost towards the perfomance of the members as it increases the personality and positive attitude of an individual.
